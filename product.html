Bio-Decomposer Program



“Our product is the Bio-Decomposer Drone, which is a solar-powered quadcopter with a sprayer compartment attached below which contains the PUSA bio-decomposer liquid. The Bio Decomposer is prepared with a mix of seven different fungi, which produce several digestive enzymes, including pectin, cellulose, and lignin in the the form of a capsules. A mixture of 25 litres can be produced with the help of 4 capsules, some jaggery, and flour made with chickpeas. Such mixture shall be sufficient to cover an area equivalent to 1 hectare of land.


When the liquid is sprayed upon the stubble, it takes about 20 days for the stubble to degrade completely and form manure. This way the farmers can use the leftover stubble as manure for the future crops and have to spend less on the chemical fertilisers which destroy the fertile soil. This also saves the farmer’s money and since the stubble is not burnt, it doesn’t contribute to pollution. This can be a big achievement for the agricultural sector.”

-Sarvesh Pahilajani
